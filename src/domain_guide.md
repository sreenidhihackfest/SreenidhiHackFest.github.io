# Domain Guide

<p style="font-size: 20px;">
Welcome to the Domain Guide! This document provides an overview of the various domains available for HACKFEST '26. Each domain represents a unique area of innovation and problem-solving.
</p>

## Domains!

1. **Web & Mobile Innovation**
   - Focus on creating innovative web and mobile applications to solve real-world problems.

2. **AI & Data Intelligence**
   - Leverage artificial intelligence and data analytics to derive insights and build intelligent systems.

3. **Smart Systems & IoT**
   - Develop solutions using Internet of Things (IoT) technologies to create smart and connected systems.

4. **Future Tech & Cybersecurity**
   - Explore cutting-edge technologies and build secure systems to address modern challenges.

5. **HealthTech**
   - Innovate in the healthcare sector using technology to improve patient care and medical processes.

6. **Green Energy & Sustainability**
   - Design solutions that promote renewable energy and sustainable practices.

7. **EdTech**
   - Create tools and platforms to enhance education and learning experiences.

8. **AgriTech**
   - Develop technologies to improve agricultural practices and food production.

---

## Domain Selection Process

- Each team must select one domain to work on during the hackathon.
- Problem statements for each domain will be provided by the organizers and sponsors.
- Domains and problem statements are available on a first-come, first-served basis.
- Once a domain is locked by a team, it cannot be selected by another team.

---

## Next Steps

1. Review the available domains and identify the one that aligns with your team's interests and expertise.
2. Lock your domain as soon as possible to secure your preferred choice.
3. Stay tuned for the release of problem statements, which will be updated in this guide.

---

We encourage you to explore the domains and choose one that inspires your creativity and innovation. Good luck!
