## Hackathon Preparation

### For Beginners

Hackathons are a great place to learn, build, and network. Here are a few tips to get you started:

-   **Team Up:** Join a team to learn from others and share the workload.
-   **Brainstorm Ideas:** Think about real-world problems you'd like to solve.
-   **Learn the Basics:** Familiarize yourself with Git for collaboration.
-   **Set Up Your Environment:** Install necessary software before the event.
-   **Define Your MVP:** Focus on a Minimum Viable Product - the core functionality of your idea.
-   **Don't Be Afraid to Ask for Help:** Mentors are there to guide you.
-   **Have Fun!**

For a more detailed guide, check out this [Hackathon Preparation Guide for Beginners](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/hackathon-preparation-guide-for-beginners/).

-   **BHASHINI:** [bhashini.gov.in](https://bhashini.gov.in/en/explore) - An AI-driven language translation platform with APIs.
