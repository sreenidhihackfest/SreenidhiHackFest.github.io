## Evaluation Tracks & Flow

Evaluation process varies based on the track the team picks:

-   **Hard Problem Track:** Solutions are evaluated at the end of Day 2.
-   **Easy Problem Track:** Teams submit two solutions. The first is evaluated at the end of Day 1, and the second at the end of Day 2.

All projects will be judged on technical merit, innovation, and overall execution.

---

## Judging Criteria

Judges will score projects based on the following general criteria:

1.  **Innovation & Creativity:** How original is the idea?
2.  **Technical Implementation:** Does the solution work effectively?
3.  **Impact & Feasibility:** Does the project address a meaningful problem?
4.  **Presentation:** How well is the project demonstrated?

Winners will be announced at the closing ceremony. Good luck!
